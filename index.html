Ashish Drug Agencies App Support

Welcome to the Support Page for the Ashish Drug Agencies App

Ashish Drug Agencies is dedicated to providing the best tools for our salesmen and employees to manage orders, supply, and payment collection efficiently. This app is designed exclusively for the internal functioning of our firm and is a crucial part of our operations.

Key Features of the App

  • Order Management: Salesmen can send orders directly from the field to the office, ensuring a seamless and efficient order processing system.
  • Supply Management System: Keep track of inventory and supply levels, manage restocking, and streamline the supply chain process.
  • Payment Collection: Facilitate and record payment collections directly within the app, making the payment process more straightforward and efficient.

Common Issues and Solutions

1. Unable to Log In

Solution: Ensure you are using the correct credentials provided by Ashish Drug Agencies. If you have forgotten your password, please contact the IT department at ashishdrugagencies@gmail.com for assistance.

2. App Crashes or Freezes

Solution: Try restarting your device and reopening the app. Ensure that you have the latest version of the app installed. If the issue persists, please report the problem by contacting our support team.

3. Issues with Order Submission

Solution: Verify your internet connection and try resubmitting the order. If the problem continues, please contact our support team with the order details so we can investigate the issue.
